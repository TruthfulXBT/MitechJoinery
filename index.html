Our Story

Family craft,
built to last

Mitech Joinery Limited was founded with a single commitment — to manufacture and install wooden gates of exceptional quality, built the right way, to last a lifetime. Based in Ripley, Derbyshire, we have spent over 34 years perfecting our craft.

What began as a traditional joinery business has grown into one of the UK’s leading specialists in bespoke wooden and aluminium automated gates. Every gate that leaves our workshop is made to measure — designed around your home, your entrance and your vision.

We are proud to remain a family business at heart. No call centres, no subcontractors — just Robert and his team, delivering a personal service from first conversation to final handover.

Our Journey

34 years of
craftsmanship

From a small joinery workshop in Derbyshire to one of the UK’s most respected bespoke gate manufacturers — here’s how we got here.

1990

Founded in Derbyshire

Mitech Joinery established in Ripley, Derbyshire, specialising in traditional timber joinery and bespoke wooden gates.

Late 1990s

Automation Expertise

Expanded into gate automation, becoming one of the first traditional joinery firms to offer full electric gate installation in the Midlands.

2000s

Aluminium & Accoya Range

Introduced aluminium and Accoya gate ranges, widening our offering to include the most durable and low-maintenance materials available.

2010s

National Growth

Grew our installation footprint across the UK, delivering and fitting gates from Derbyshire to London and beyond, while keeping the personal service of a family business.

Today

1,000+ Gates & Counting

Over a thousand installations across the UK, a 5-star reputation, and the same commitment to quality that Robert started with over 34 years ago.
